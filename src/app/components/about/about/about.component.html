Welcome to Town Builder.

The revolutionary video game that teaches front-end development to kids.
Developed on thorough research into children's learning, we want to empower the next generation.

SEE OUR CLASSROOM RESOURCES

Meet Mayor Valery.

She will be joining you on your quest to learn industry-leading skills in a creative and supportive environment.

TownBuilder provides students with extensive resources to design and build their own unique world through HTML, CSS and Javascript.

LEARN

Learn through our comprehensive IDE and see the power of front-end development with our live-updating preview.

EXPLORE

Join Mayor Valery on her quest to build her quiet home into a town.

Meet quirky characters and bring their dreams to life, integrating them into your town.

BUILD

With 100's of premade assets, you can design and create the buildings of your dreams.

Once you're comfortable with the environment, you can go off-story and start your own timeline.
